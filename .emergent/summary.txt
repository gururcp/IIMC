<analysis>**original_problem_statement:** 
The user wants a simple application to track the progress of a construction project. The project has 138 items detailed in an uploaded PDF.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
1.  **Task Tracking**: Track progress for 138 project items in percentages.
2.  **Reporting**: Show actual vs. planned progress and identify at-risk items.
3.  **Hierarchy & Rollup**: Support parent-child task relationships. Progress updates on child tasks must automatically roll up to parent tasks.
4.  **Update Restriction**: Only allow progress updates on the lowest-level tasks (leaf nodes).
5.  **Phases**: Group tasks into logical phases.
6.  **Default Status**: All tasks should default to Not Started.
7.  **Gantt Chart**: A visual, interactive Gantt chart to display timelines and dependencies.
8.  **Report Generation**: Generate daily, weekly, and monthly reports in Excel or PDF format showing task statuses (completed, in-progress, delayed, at-risk).
9.  **Editable Dates**: Allow editing of start and end dates for tasks.
10. **Update History**: Maintain and display a history of all updates made to a task.
11. **Ease of Use**: Task updating should be extremely simple and accessible (at your fingertips) without complex navigation.

**User's preferred language**: en
